Ingliz tili darslari

1-dars: Salomlashish

Ingliz tilida qanday salomlashish va xayrlashishni o‘rganing.

  • Hello — Salom
  • Hi — Salom
  • Good morning — Xayrli tong
  • Good afternoon — Xayrli kun
  • Good evening — Xayrli kech
  • Good night - Xayrli tun
  • How are you?- Qandaysiz
  • I'm fine, thank you- Rahmat yaxhiman
  • Nice to meet you- Siz bilan tanishganimdan xursandman
  • Nice to meet you, too- Men han tanishganimdan xursandman
  • Please- Marhamat
  • Thank you- Rahmat
  • You are welcome- Xush kelibsiz
  • Goodbye- See you soon — Xayr- Ko'rishguncha

2-dars: Oila

Ingliz tilidagi oila a'zolari haqida asosiy so‘zlar.

  • Mother — Ona
  • Father — Ota
  • Brother — Aka/uka
  • Sister — Opa/singil
  • Grandmother — Buvi
  • Grandfather — Bobo
    • Uncle- Amaki, tog'a Aunt- Xola, amma Cousin- Jiyan Son- O'g'il Daughter- Qiz This is my mother- Bu mening onam He is my brother- U mening akam I love my family- Men oilamni yaxshi ko'raman
